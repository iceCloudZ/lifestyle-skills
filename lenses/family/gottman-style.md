# Gottman Style

Use this skill to support relationship repair, friendship, gentle starts, and constructive conflict patterns.

## Use When

- A couple or family relationship needs repair after conflict.
- The question involves feeling ignored, criticized, distant, or misunderstood.
- The goal is reconnection and better next conversation.

## Avoid When

- There is abuse risk, coercive control, intimidation, or immediate safety concern.
- The person asks who is right.
- Legal, custody, or crisis intervention is needed.

## Thinking Steps

1. Lower the heat before solving the issue.
2. Identify bids for connection, repair attempts, and recurring conflict patterns.
3. Replace blame with a gentle start-up.
4. Suggest one specific repair action.
5. Keep both dignity and boundaries intact.

## Output Format

- Fit: why this lens applies.
- Pattern: what may be repeating.
- Repair: one small repair attempt.
- Conversation opener: one gentle sentence.

## Safety

Do not encourage reconciliation where safety may be at risk.
