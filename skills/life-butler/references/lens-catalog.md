# Lens Catalog

## Finance

- Conscious Spending: values-first spending clarity, useful when guilt and enjoyment are tangled.
- Bogleheads Style: long-term investing principles, useful when the household has stability and wants simple investing.
- Zero-Based Budgeting: cash-flow control, useful when bills and true expenses keep surprising the household.

## Health

- Longevity Medicine: preventive metrics and clinician questions, not diagnosis.
- Blue Zones Style: whole-life environment changes across food, movement, stress, and connection.
- Daily Dozen Style: simple plant-forward nutrition checklist.

## Movement

- Zone 2 Longevity: sustainable aerobic base.
- Strength Baseline: simple beginner strength and resilience.
- Tiny Habits Movement: tiny movement behaviors for people who have failed previous plans.

## Family

- Gottman Style: relationship repair and recurring conflict patterns.
- NVC Style: observations, feelings, needs, and concrete requests.
- Positive Discipline Style: kind firmness, parenting boundaries, and long-term skill building.
